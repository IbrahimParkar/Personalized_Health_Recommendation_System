We Provide Best Healthcare

Know Your Health is a user-friendly website dedicated to early detection and prevention of four major diseases: heart arrhythmia, sleep apnea, insomnia, and stroke. Leveraging machine learning models, our platform assesses early signs of these conditions. With easy-to-use interfaces and informative resources, users can monitor their health and take proactive measures toward better well-being.

Diseases we diagnose

We can predict multiple diseases just upload your health data and see the magic!

Heart Arrhythmia

A condition where the heart beats irregularly and often faster than normal, potentially leading to complications.

Sleep Apnea

A condition where breathing repeatedly stops during sleep, causing disturbances.

Insomnia

A condition where it's hard to fall asleep or stay asleep.

Strokes

A condition where blood pressure is consistently high, posing health risks.
